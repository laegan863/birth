Possible Issues when Applying for Your Birth Certificate

Applicants seeking a U.S. birth certificate may face some challenges. If you apply through VitalCert, our experienced team will assist you in addressing and resolving any issues that might arise during the process.

Lost original birth certificate

This is a common reason why someone might need a copy. If you don't have the original birth certificate, you'll need alternative proof of identity to successfully apply for a replacement. Keep in mind that each state has different rules about which documents are accepted.

Incorrect information on birth certificate

On rare occasions, applicants might receive a copy of a birth certificate with incorrect information. This could involve errors in names, dates, or other vital details. If this happens, applicants should promptly contact Vital Records, and we will work quickly to correct the issue.

State department questions legitimacy of birth certificate

Occasionally, the state department might question the validity of a birth certificate for various reasons, such as when a birth isn't officially registered on time after a home birth.
